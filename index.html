Who We Are

Safer world focuses on plastic-eating bacteria as a way to curb environmental pollution. We hope to research and support other researchers who are focusing on developing plastic eating bacteria. This organization is necessary because most of the current companies and organizations seem to be ineffective in curbing the rates of plastic pollution. This can be confirmed by an article by UNEP. Our organization innovates because it does not just suggest a solution: Plastic eating bacteria, but also, take advantage of marketing and social media to promote the solution through competitions.
